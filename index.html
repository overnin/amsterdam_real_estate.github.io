Amsterdam Real Estate Explorer

The map below shows as dots the current property for sale in amsterdam. The colored areas are an average price of sold properties. The properties and areas are following the same color scale in order to contextualize the property price. By pointing on the properties or the areas, the price per square meter value appears. By zooming or navigating around the city, the scale adjusts to the visible part of the map: 3 zones are colored to identify the low, medium, high areas. As the map sets on an area, different statistics and graphs are compute to properties contained this visible part.
